Install Mi Fit from the Google Play Store

1. In the Google Play Store search for Mi Fit and then click on Install.

2. Once installed, click Open.

3. You need to log into the Mi Fit system. I have set up an account for you. Enter the email address: davidemail2@email.com and the password that I emailed to you. Then tap, Sign in.

4. You now need to approve a couple of requests.

5. The next stage is to pair (i.e. connect) the band to your phone. (Make sure the band is close to your phone.) Tap the orange bar "...tap to pair", then the blue rectangle labelled "Band", click "Agree" and now Mi Fit will try to find your band. If it is successful, the band will vibrate and you will need to click the band to confirm that you wish to pair it. (You might need your glasses for this bit because the message on the band is very small.)

6. If the pairing has worked, you will be shown a series of images of what you can do with your band. Just swipe each one to the right until you get to the Try Now button, and click it.

7. You should now see a screen that looks like this. Scroll down to see all the rows. The settings should all be correct but I have ringed the important ones. If they are different, please change them by tapping on each one.

8. Tap the left arrow at the top of the screen to go back to the main screen, then click the Profile button. Now scroll down to the bottom and click Settings.

9. Make sure "Show status in Notification shade" is on. If not, tap it to turn it on.

Mi Fit should now be set up.

Install my app, Cyfer Health Client, using the invitation link

10. You now need to set up my app, Cyfer Health Client. The following steps shold be a lot more straightforward.

11. On your phone, open the email I sent you with the invitation and tap the link.

12. You may be asked to log in to your Google account.

13. Once logged in, you will see the invitation to access my app. Scroll down to find the Accept button, and click it.

14. You should then get the Welcome message, Scroll down and click the blue text to download my app.

15. You may be asked to install the app, if so, click on the Install button. Once installed, click the Open button. You should now be running my app.

16. You will be asked to allow my app to know your phone's location. Click Allow. Now whenever you wear the band, you should see the heart rate being display in the middle of the screen. (It may take a minute or two before you start seeing the readings.)

17. If you want to shut-down my app, tap the cog symbol and then the no-entry button.
